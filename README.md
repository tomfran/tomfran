### Relevant projects
My most relevant projects are the following: 
- [Urban sound classification with neural networks](https://github.com/tomfran/urban-sound-classification): the goal is to perform multi-class classification on the UrbanSound8k dataset, using MLPs and CNNs.
- [Last.fm user listenings analysis](https://github.com/tomfran/lastfm-project): the goal here is to collect data from the Last.fm api, storing it in a datalake fashion on Google Cloud platform. The analysis part is written in PySpark.
- [Caselaw temporal analysis](https://github.com/tomfran/caselaw-temporal-analysis): the goal here is to develop a methodology to stydy legal text with Information Retrieval techniques, the main ones used are topic modelling and word embeddings.
- [Drones distributed system](https://github.com/tomfran/dronazon): the idea is to simulate a distributed system that makes deliveries. It covers many distributed systems problems such as election, sensors data collection and processing, concurrency and building an api.
